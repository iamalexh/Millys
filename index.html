About Millys

 

Millys Bed and Breakfast in picturesque Polperro is an ideal location for your holiday or weekend break. Situated on the main street leading into the idyllic fishing village of Polperro this restricted traffic zone area provides peace and tranquility for all guests.

Surrounded by beautiful countryside in a steep wooded valley guests may explore the village's narrow winding lanes, delightful harbour, interesting shops, galleries and typical Cornish pubs.

 

Soak up the sun, rest, relax, renew, get your feet wet, take long walks, enjoy the fresh air, smile, giggle and laugh.
